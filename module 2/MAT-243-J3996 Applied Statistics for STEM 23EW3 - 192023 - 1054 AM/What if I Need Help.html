Academic Support has you covered. Open the Academic Support in any of your courses, and click on "Get Help with Your Schoolwork." Browse options like live workshops, drop-off paper review, video resources, and 1:1 tutoring.

In the same module, you will also find a link to the Shapiro Library, where you can access research databases and APA/MLA citation guides, and connect with librarians who are ready to assist you.
